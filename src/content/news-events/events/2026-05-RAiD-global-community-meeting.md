## Overview

Join the RAiD - Research Activity Identifier - global meeting to hear updates from around the world at a time friendly to you.

Over three chase-the-sun webinars, we will share an update on RAiD (Research Activity Identifier), an international standard (ISO 23527:2022), persistent identifier, and global registry for identifying, tracking and sharing research projects, developed by the Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC).

We will share updates from the Australian Research Data Commons on the global state of RAiD as well as the Australian ARDC RAiD Service.

We will be joined by a number of guest speakers who will discuss how RAiD links with other systems.

This session will be recorded and shared with all who register.

## Who should attend?

Researchers, academics, university librarians, researchers who code, research data specialists, digital infrastructure providers, research support professionals, data custodians, digital research trainers.

## What will participants gain from the session?

Attendees will gain a better understanding of RAiD and the work ahead.

## Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, the sessions will be recorded. The recording will be provided to all registrants and published on the ARDC YouTube channel.
